Adventures in Aqaba

If you are looking for excitement, Aqaba has it all...

Sunset Cruise on the Red Sea

Enjoy the picturesque sunset over the mountains of Jordan, Eilat and Taba during a one-hour Red Sea cruise aboard one of the modern boats of Sindbad Water Sports company.

Indulge yourself with sunset cruise or try the delights of a barbeque at sea. If you opt for the sunset excursion, do not forget your camera; your friends back home will never believe your descriptions of Aqaba's spectacular end of day beauty.

Parasailing Adventure

Take a breath-taking adventure and try parasailing fly high with family and friends, while watching the panoramic scenery of the Red Sea.

For more information and booking, please contact us:

Phone: +962 3 203 40 20

Wadi Rum - Desert Camel Tour

Camels are surely one of the most authentic ways to see the desert although they are not always the most comfortable means of travel. A camel trek really puts you at the heart of the desert experience. Most treks are just for the day, but if you find you have adapted happily to the slow gait of the camel, you can arrange a longer excursion to either Aqaba or Petra.

Wadi Rum - Desert Hike

Colour-shifting landforms, giant sandstone mountains, towering plateaus, steep canyons, mushroom rocks and perpetually moving sand dunes are just some of the elements of Wadi Rum's splendour. A desert with incredibly beautiful sandstone formations, made famous from movies such as “Lawrence of Arabia” and “Indiana Jones”. A visit to Wadi Rum when on holiday in Aqaba is a “must”, and spending the night in one of the camps there is definitely an unforgettable experience. All hiking tours must be arranged through Wadi Rum Visitor's Centre and an official Bedouin guide who will either accompany you or help you organise your hike.
